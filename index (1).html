Project 02 Thermal Analysis

Heat Transfer Numerical Study

Finite-difference modeling and SolidWorks thermal validation

Modeled long-fin heat conduction numerically using MATLAB finite-difference methods with iterative SOR solvers. Results were cross-validated against SolidWorks Thermal Analysis to confirm accuracy and identify solver convergence behavior under varying boundary conditions.

Finite-Difference Method

Implemented MATLAB-based long-fin conduction model using a structured FD discretization.

SOR Solver

Applied iterative Successive Over-Relaxation to achieve convergence across the temperature field.

SolidWorks Validation

Validated numerical results against SolidWorks Thermal Analysis to verify model accuracy.

  • Formulated finite-difference equations for long-fin conduction and implemented in MATLAB.
  • Applied SOR iterative solving to achieve numerical convergence across the thermal domain.
  • Compared MATLAB outputs to SolidWorks Thermal Analysis results to evaluate model fidelity.
  • Documented solver behavior and convergence characteristics across boundary condition variations.
Project 03 Experimental Testing

Wind Tunnel Testing of Vehicle Model

Aerodynamic drag measurement and CFD comparison on a 3D-printed model

Manufactured a 3D-printed scale vehicle model and tested it in a wind tunnel to measure drag and characterize airflow. Experimental results were compared against computational simulations to evaluate design accuracy and validate performance predictions.

3D Print Model manufactured in-house for wind tunnel testing
LabVIEW Airflow data acquisition and measurement platform
MATLAB Post-processing and validation against analytical results
  • Designed and 3D-printed a scale vehicle model to controlled dimensional tolerances for wind tunnel use.
  • Measured drag forces and airflow velocity profiles across test conditions using LabVIEW.
  • Processed experimental data in MATLAB and compared against simulated predictions.
  • Evaluated accuracy of CFD model against physical test results and documented performance gaps.

Engineering Takeaway

Physical testing revealed discrepancies between CFD predictions and measured drag, identifying opportunities to refine mesh resolution and boundary condition assumptions in the simulation model.
